ELECTRICITY PM UPDATE

Apr 16 2013 5:06PM

Energy prices ($/MWh) in the northeast were mixed; NYISO Zone G which did gain slightly in the May - June time period; MW prices remain supported Y-o-Y due to non-gas plant seasonal outages and specifically in June due to a planned maintenance outage on AGT pipeline; trading activity continues to remain subdued The gas/coal spread referenced against Central Appalachian prices remains substantially higher Y-o-Y and implies that gas fired plants, which typically set energy prices, are the driver behind energy prices paid to all generators in the PJM/NYISO/ISO-NE regions May pricing in all regions is being supported by concerns over seasonal plant outages mid-Apr through mid-May; this increases the potential increase of gas fired generation at higher Yo-Y gas prices read more >>
